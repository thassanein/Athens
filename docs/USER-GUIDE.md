# Athens Facility Compliance — Field Guide

A one-page guide to using the app on your phone. **App address:** https://athens-compliance.onrender.com

---

## Signing in
1. Open the link above on your phone.
2. Enter the **passcode** your supervisor gave you, tap **Enter**.
3. *(Recommended)* Add it to your home screen so it opens like an app:
   - **iPhone (Safari):** Share button → **Add to Home Screen**
   - **Android (Chrome):** ⋮ menu → **Add to Home screen**

> First load after a quiet period can take ~30–60 seconds to wake up — that's normal.

---

## The bottom menu
Five buttons along the bottom of every screen:

| Button | What it's for |
|---|---|
| 🗺 **Map** | Home. All facilities on a map + a snapshot of open findings, items to verify, and what's due. Tap a site to open it. |
| ✓ **Tasks** | Your "My Day" list — every open finding across all sites. Filter by Failing, Unassigned, etc. Tap one to open it. |
| ➕ **(red button)** | **Log a new finding** from where you're standing (see below). |
| 🔔 **Alerts** | What needs attention now — findings with no owner, permits to verify, renewals coming up. |
| 👤 **Profile** | Your name/role, app settings, data source, and **Sign out**. |

---

## Logging a finding (the red ➕ button)
This is the main thing you'll do in the field.

1. Tap the **red ➕** button at the bottom.
2. Fill in the slide-up form:
   - **Site** — which facility you're at
   - **Checklist** — ENV or Facility
   - **Area** — where on site (Scale/Entrance, Ponds, HazMat, etc.)
   - **What's wrong** — describe it
   - **Owner / Due** — optional
   - **Add photo evidence** — snap a photo right there
3. Tap **Log finding.** It's saved and instantly visible to the whole team.

*Everyone (editors and viewers) can log new findings.*

---

## Working a finding
- Open any site (from the Map or Tasks), then use the tabs: **Findings · Permits · Leases · Facility · ENV**.
- Tap a finding card to expand it.
- **If you're an editor (auditor):** you can add a comment, attach/replace a photo, set the owner and due date, and change the status (**Pass / Open / Fail / N-A**).
- **If you're a viewer:** you see all the details read-only (and can still log new findings with the ➕).

The site screen also shows a **site map** with permit and finding pins, and a **"next 90 days"** ribbon for upcoming renewals. Permit and lease cards are tap-to-expand for full details.

---

## Two access levels
- **Editor (Auditor)** — can change findings (comments, photos, status, owner/due).
- **Viewer** — read-only on existing findings, but can still capture new ones.

Your level is set by which passcode you were given. Profile shows a **"Can edit"** or **"Read-only"** badge.

---

## Good to know
- **Shared data:** everyone sees the same information — your updates show up for the whole team.
- **Sign out** is under **Profile** when you're done on a shared device.
- Trouble signing in? Double-check the passcode (it's case-sensitive) and your internet connection.
